Our Story

Kind Heart Kenya - Transforming Lives Across Africa

Founded in Kenya, Kind Heart is dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children across Africa. We believe every child deserves access to education, healthcare, and a safe environment to grow and thrive.

Our Mission

To provide essential support and create sustainable opportunities for African children in need.

  • Quality Education
  • Healthcare Access
  • Nutrition Programs

Children Helped

Stella Kasiva

Executive Director

Stella founded Kind Heart Kenya after witnessing the struggles of children in rural communities. With over 10 years of experience in community development, she leads our efforts to create sustainable change.

"Every child deserves a chance to dream and achieve. At Kind Heart, we're turning those dreams into reality, one child at a time."
